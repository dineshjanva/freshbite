Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our service

How fast is your delivery service?

We guarantee delivery within 30 minutes for all orders within 3 miles of our partner restaurants. For areas beyond this radius, delivery typically takes 30-45 minutes. If we're late, your meal is on us!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash on delivery. All online payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

Do you offer catering for events?

Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. Our catering menu features crowd-pleasing options from our partner restaurants. Please contact our catering team at catering@freshbite.com or call +1 (555) 987-6543 at least 48 hours in advance for orders.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed and dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track your order in real-time through our mobile app. Our live tracking shows your rider's location and estimated arrival time.

What is your refund policy?

If you're unsatisfied with your order for any reason, contact us within 24 hours for a full refund. For incorrect or missing items, we'll either redeliver the correct items or refund the amount immediately. All refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.

Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Absolutely! We offer a wide range of vegetarian and vegan options across all our partner restaurants. You can filter by dietary preferences on our menu page. All vegan items are clearly marked with a plant-based icon for easy identification.
