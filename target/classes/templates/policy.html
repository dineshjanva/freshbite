Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

Last updated: August 15, 2023

Policy Sections

Introduction What Data We Collect How We Use Your Data Data Sharing Cookies & Tracking Data Security Your Privacy Rights Policy Changes Contact Us

1. Introduction

Welcome to FreshBite ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate the FreshBite mobile application and website (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access or use the Service.

Key Points:

  • We collect personal data to provide and improve our Service
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • You have rights regarding your personal data
  • We implement security measures to protect your information

2. What Data We Collect

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Delivery address and location data
  • Payment information (credit card details processed securely)
  • Account credentials (username and password)
  • Order history and preferences

Usage Data

We collect information on how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This may include:

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, device type)
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages
  • Search queries, order preferences
  • Crash reports and performance data

Location Data

We may collect your location data to provide location-based services such as delivery tracking and restaurant recommendations. You can enable or disable location services through your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for various purposes:

Order Processing

To process and deliver your food orders, including payment processing and delivery coordination.

Account Management

To create and manage your account, provide customer support, and communicate with you.

Marketing & Promotions

To send promotional offers, newsletters, and updates about our services (you can opt-out anytime).

Service Improvement

To analyze usage patterns, improve our service, and develop new features.

We may also use your information for legal compliance, fraud prevention, and to enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following situations:

With Service Providers

We share data with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Delivery partners and drivers
  • Restaurant partners for order fulfillment
  • Cloud storage providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Analytics and marketing platforms

For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information where required by law, to protect our rights, or to comply with legal processes.

Business Transfers

In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition, your information may be transferred.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service and hold certain information.

Type Purpose Examples
Essential Cookies Necessary for site functionality Session management, authentication
Preference Cookies Remember your preferences Language settings, dietary preferences
Analytics Cookies Understand usage patterns Google Analytics, Mixpanel
Marketing Cookies Deliver relevant advertising Facebook Pixel, Google Ads

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service.

6. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data and implement appropriate measures to protect it:

Encryption

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ protocols

Access Control

Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms

Secure Storage

Data stored in secure facilities with regular backups

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

You can request copies of your personal data.

Right to Rectification

You can request correction of inaccurate data.

Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data.

Right to Restrict Processing

You can request restriction of data processing.

Right to Data Portability

You can request transfer of your data to another organization.

Right to Object

You can object to certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the "Contact Us" section below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

We will let you know via email and/or a prominent notice on our Service prior to the change becoming effective. The "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy indicates when it was last revised.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when they are posted on this page.
