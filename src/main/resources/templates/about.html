Our Humble Beginnings

From a small kitchen to serving thousands daily, our journey has been fueled by passion and quality.

2018

The Idea Was Born

Founders Sarah and Michael started FreshBite with a simple idea: deliver restaurant-quality meals faster than anyone else. Their first kitchen was a small 300 sq ft space in downtown New York.

2020

Expansion & Growth

During the pandemic, we expanded to 5 cities and partnered with 50+ local restaurants. Our delivery network grew to 200+ riders ensuring food arrives fresh and hot.

2022

Technology Innovation

Launched our AI-powered app with predictive ordering and real-time tracking. Received the "Tech Innovation in Food" award for our sustainable packaging solutions.

2023

Today & Beyond

Serving 20+ cities with 500+ restaurant partners and 1000+ delivery riders. Our mission remains the same: deliver happiness, one meal at a time.
