1. Delivery Areas

We deliver to most locations within our service zones. Our delivery coverage is expanding regularly, so please check back often if we're not currently in your area.

Current Coverage

We currently serve these areas:

  • Downtown New York (10001-10007)
  • Midtown Manhattan (10016-10022)
  • Brooklyn Heights (11201)
  • Williamsburg (11211, 11249)
  • Long Island City (11101)

Expansion Areas

We're working to expand to these areas soon:

  • Queens (11354, 11355, 11356)
  • Upper East Side (10021, 10028)
  • Upper West Side (10023, 10024)
  • Harlem (10026, 10027)

How to check if we deliver to your address:

Enter your delivery address during checkout or use our Delivery Area Checker. If your location isn't currently covered, you can sign up to be notified when we expand to your neighborhood.

3. Delivery Fees

Our delivery fees are based on distance and order size to ensure fair compensation for our delivery partners.

Distance from Restaurant Standard Fee Express Fee
0-1 mile $1.99 $3.99
1-3 miles $2.99 $4.99
3-5 miles $3.99 $5.99
5+ miles $4.99 + $0.50/mile Not available

Free Delivery Opportunities

  • Orders over $30 (before tax)
  • First-time customers
  • Membership program subscribers
  • During promotional periods

Service Fee

A small service fee (5% of order value, max $3) applies to all orders to help maintain our platform and support our delivery partners.

4. Order Minimums

Standard Minimum

Most restaurants require a $10 minimum order before taxes and fees.

Premium Restaurants

Some premium restaurants may require a higher minimum, typically $15-$25.

No Minimum Orders:

During promotional periods and for members of our FreshBite Prime program, minimum order requirements are waived. Check our app for current promotions.

6. Resolving Delivery Issues

Late Delivery

If your order is more than 30 minutes late and qualifies under our guarantee policy, we'll issue a credit for your next order.

How to report: Contact us within 30 minutes of delivery completion.

Missing/Incorrect Items

If items are missing or incorrect, contact us immediately. We'll arrange for the correct items to be delivered or issue a refund.

How to report: Contact us within 30 minutes of delivery.

Food Temperature Issues

If your food arrives at an unsatisfactory temperature, contact us immediately. We'll resolve the issue to your satisfaction.

Damaged Packaging

If your order arrives with damaged packaging, contact us with photos. We'll issue a credit or replacement.

7. Delivery Instructions

Provide clear delivery instructions to ensure a smooth delivery experience.

Helpful Instructions

Good

"Leave at front door. Building code: 1234#. Please don't ring bell."

Avoid

"Leave at door" (without specifying which door or access instructions)

Special Requests

For special delivery requests:

  • Contactless delivery: Available by default
  • Leave at door: Specify location details
  • Hand to me: Specify who should receive
  • Gate codes: Provide full codes with instructions
  • Building access: Provide necessary information
